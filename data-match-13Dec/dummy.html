The Framework

Empathy Map: Provides an emotional connection to the Business Decision Maker (BDM), enabling personalized design, communication, and nudging.

Business Plan: Enables programmatic assessment of business intent, allowing efficient triage by sector, maturity, geography, etc.

Value Proposition: Helps evaluate the likelihood of business success programmatically, identifying improvable areas based on pain/gain matches.

Business Model Canvas: Highlights specific needs (resources, geography, etc.) that can guide targeted support or market matchmaking.

Opportunities for DBT Support: Detailed interviews reveal opportunities for support based on business descriptions.
